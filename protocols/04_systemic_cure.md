### 1. The Concept: Uncertainty Architecture

We are proposing a new architectural standard for governing AI. Instead of treating AI as a "magic box" that writes code, we must treat it as an **untrusted subsystem** that requires a dedicated **Control Plane**.

**The Control Plane must:**
1.  **Contain:** Isolate AI-generated logic from core business rules.
2.  **Verify:** Automated, deterministic checks that run *before* human review.
3.  **Limit:** Hard constraints on the "Blast Radius" of an AI agent.
